Brief description

The domestic cat (felis catus) is a small, typically furry, carnivorous mammal often kept as a household pet. They walk on four legs and they have retractable claws in their paws. Their hind legs are more powerful and the claws are more thick. They are known to be agile, intelligent and solitary hunters with keen senses. They communicate with body language, meowing, and purring.

Fun Facts

  • Run up to 30 miles per hour
  • Jump 5 times their height!
  • Sleep 12-16 hours daily
  • They have a unique nose print

Short Poem about cats

A silent shadow on the floor,
With velvet paws and emerald eyes,
They watch the birds outside the door,
And wait to claim their cozy prize.

A sudden purr, a gentle stretch,
They curl into a sleeping ring,
A sunbeam is the spot they fetch,
While dreams of hunting, soft, they bring.
